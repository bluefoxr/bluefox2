About

High-quality data analysis is at the core of evidence-based policy making.

Data has never been more abundant and readily available, and this presents huge opportunities for policy making. But translating data into relevant information requires skill and experience. At the same time, policy making is intertwined with complex multidimensional concepts, such as sustainable development, innovation, connectivity, and many others. BlueFox offers data-driven indicator solutions, built on years of experience and collaborations in composite indicator development, international data analysis, policy making and statistics. Read more

What is BlueFox?

BlueFox is a consultancy service launched by Dr. William Becker. William has a PhD in mechanical engineering, machine learning and statistics, and has worked for the last decade on many high-level projects linked to indicators and policy.

Specific areas of expertise and research include composite indicators, sustainable development, international connectivity, performance-based budgeting uncertainty/sensitivity analysis, and many more. William has worked with the European Commission, the UN, and many other international organisations, and is considered a leading expert in composite indicators.
